### Global Cryptocurrency Adoption Trends.

#### What is the problem you are solving?
Cryptocurrency is becoming more popular around the world, but it is not always clear why some countries adopt it faster than others. Information about crypto adoption, internet access, inflation, and economic conditions is available in different places, which makes it hard to see the full picture. This project will combine these datasets to better understand global cryptocurrency trends and the factors that influence its adoption.

#### Tools to use
* Excel for data storage and exploration
* Power Query for data cleaning and transformation
* Tableau for visualization and dashboard creation


#### Tell your audience how you got the answer, tools show your capability
I will collect data from several reliable sources such as Chainalysis, CoinGecko, the World Bank, and the IMF. These datasets will include information about cryptocurrency adoption, market prices, internet access, inflation, and other economic indicators from different countries.

I will organize the data in Excel and use Power Query to clean and prepare it. This will involve removing missing values, making country names consistent, and combining the datasets using common fields like country and year.

I will therefore import my data set into Tableau to create visualizations and dashboards. These visuals will show the patterns and understand how factors like inflation, internet access, and economic conditions may influence cryptocurrency adoption.


#### What insights did you or do you want to discover?/ What Solutions do you want to offer? Do People even need these solutions?
While analysing the data, I want to find patterns that really explain why cryptocurrency adoption differs across countries. For example:
•	Are people in countries with high inflation turning to crypto as a safer store of value?
•	Does better internet access lead to higher crypto usage?
•	How do spikes in Bitcoin or Ethereum prices affect adoption trends?
The goal is to see the story behind the numbers, not just show charts


Solutions I Want to Offer

These insights can help:
•	Businesses focus on regions where crypto is growing fast.
•	Policymakers understand which factors encourage or slow adoption, helping create informed regulations.
•	Researchers and analysts have a clear picture of global trends instead of scattered data.

People Need These Solutions because,

Right now, the crypto world is fast-moving and confusing. Investors, companies, and governments struggle to understand where and why crypto is being used. This project turns scattered data into actionable insights that show real-world patterns, helping people make smarter decisions.


#### How would a business or a community (for Social Impact Projects) benefit from your work?

Businesses can use the information to target markets where cryptocurrency adoption is growing fastest. For example, a fintech company or crypto exchange could focus marketing and services on countries the adoption rate is high. They can also adjust product features based on factors like internet access or economic conditions, which improves customer satisfaction and adoption rates.
